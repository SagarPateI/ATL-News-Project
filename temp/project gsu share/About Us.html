About Us

Welcome to GSU Share, your go-to resource for free and valuable resources available to Georgia State University's students! We are a team of passionate students who believe that every student should have access to the tools and resources they need to succeed in their academic journey.

Our mission is to spread awareness about the many free resources available to Georgia State University's students, including study materials, academic support services, financial aid, career services, and more. We understand that navigating college can be challenging, especially if you are on a tight budget. That's why we are committed to providing you with all the information you need to make the most of the resources available to you.

At GSU Share, we believe that knowledge should be shared freely, and we are committed to making our website a platform for collective learning. We encourage all students to contribute to our website by sharing their experiences, resources, and insights. Together, we can create a community of learners who support each other and work towards a common goal.

We are a team of students who share a common goal: to empower our fellow students with the knowledge and tools they need to succeed. We believe that education should be accessible to all, regardless of socioeconomic status, and we are committed to providing information on the many resources available to Georgia State University's students.

Whether you are a freshman just starting your college journey, or a senior getting ready to graduate, we hope that our website will be a valuable resource for you. We are constantly updating our content to ensure that you have access to the most up-to-date information, so be sure to check back often.

Thank you for joining us on this journey, and we hope that you find our website useful. If you have any questions, feedback, or suggestions, please don't hesitate to reach out to us. We are always here to listen and support our fellow students.

We would also like to make a special thank you to all of GSU Share's contributors. Your posts are greatly appreciated. Remember: Together, we can make a positive impact on our community.
